    Application for A Madison Academic Student

      One $1000 scholarship will be awarded to a senior graduating from Madison Academic High School and one $1000 scholarship will be awarded to a senior graduating from another high school located in Madison County. Other awards may be made if funds are available.

      Requirements:

    • Accepted by either a two or four-year college or university in the state of Tennessee
    • Public Service Major (e.g., Education, Criminal Justice, Law Enforcement, Social Work, Christian or Bible Studies or the Ministry). If your major is not a traditional public service major, you may explain how you will use your education to help others.
