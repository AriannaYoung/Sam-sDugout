What is Sam's Dugout?

Sam's Dugout was put togther in honor of Sam Bomar. It is used to store school uniforms, that are then sent off to be donated to other students in the school system. His friend Ben Carlotwas one of the people responsible for putting together the dugout. It was for an Eagle Scout Project that was put together in Sam's honor, but the donating didn't start there. Ms.Donna Lane, who is still currently the crisis counselor at Madison, realised that there were students throughout district were in need of school clothes. After that, the senoir class started donating clothes for their class project.
